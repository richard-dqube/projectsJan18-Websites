The pre-registration formalities are very convenient and designed to meet international standards in relation to safety and documentation and to protect the interest of owners/buyers registering their vessels. In order to enter the Otho Register a vessel must be less than 20 years of age and must meet safety standards of the Registry.

    Otho Register permits registration of vessels under construction, new buildings and Change of flags. It permits registration of vessels involved in international, coastal or domestic trading as well as all types of off-shore vessels and vessels in lay-up.
