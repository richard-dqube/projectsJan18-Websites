Circulars



Marine Circulars are issued by the Maritime Safety and Environment Protection Department of the Otho Register or by the Ship Registry Administrator to promulgate the Flag Administration policy and national requirements on Marine Safety, Security and Environmental Protection issues and other related matters not otherwise specifically addressed by the Maritime Regulations.

The List of the Marine Circulars is given below

1. MARINE CIRCULAR 12-001 – UNITED STATES COMPREHENSIVE IRAN SANCTIONS, ACCOUNTABILITY AND DIVESTMENT ACT 2010

2. MARINE CIRCULAR 12-002 – UNITED NATIONS SANCTIONS AGAINST THE ISLAMIC REPUBLIC OF IRAN

3. MARINE CIRCULAR 12-003 – INTERIM GUIDANCE TO PALAU FLAGGED VESSELS ON THE USE OF ARMED GUARDS TO DEFEND AGAINST THE THREAT OF PIRACY

4. MARINE CIRCULAR 12-004 – OFAC COMPLIANCE CHECKLIST

5. MARINE CIRCULAR 12-005 – MLC, 2006 IN FORCE AUGUST 2013

6. MARINE CIRCULAR 12-006 – PARIS, TOKYO, INDIAN OCEAN AND BLACK SEA MOU CIC

7. MARINE CIRCULAR 12-007 – PROCEDURES FOR APPROVAL OF ARMED GUARDS COMPANIES ONBOARD PALAU VESSELS

8. MARINE CIRCULAR 12-008 – PROCEDURES FOR APPROVAL OF ARMED GUARDS PERSONEL ONBOARD PALAU VESSELS

9. MARINE CIRCULAR 12-009 – DETENTION POLICY FOR PALAU VESSELS
